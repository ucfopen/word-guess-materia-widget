Word Bank

How To Play

Fill in the missing words and bring the passages back to life.

Drag and Drop

Drag and Drop the word into where you think it belongs.

Return a word to the bank by clicking it or dragging it back into its previous spot.

Tap / Click

Tap / Click the word once to select it, or to return it to the bank.

When a word is selected, tap / click it again to place it in the desired spot.

Control Function

Tab

Navigate between words on screen.

Enter

Press once to select a word. Press again to place it into its destination.

Once a word is selected, use the arrow keys to cycle through the spots.

H

Toggle the instructions.

R

Reset the selected word.

Ctrl

+

Reset all words.
