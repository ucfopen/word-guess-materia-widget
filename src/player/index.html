How To Play

Drag and Drop

Drag and Drop the word into where you think it belongs. Return a word to the bank by clicking it or dragging it back into its previous spot.

Tap / Click

Tap or Click the word once to select it or to return it to the bank
When a word is selected tap or click it again to place it in the spot where you think it belongs

Control Function

Tab

Navigate between words on screen

Enter
